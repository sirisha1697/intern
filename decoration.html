About ISTS
 International School of Technology and Sciences (ISTS) for Women is a private engineering college located at Rajahmundry in East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. The college is affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college offers undergraduate courses in engineering in three branches. International School of Technology & Sciences (ISTS) offers a global approach to education and research, with a focus on international perspectives and expertise Departments: ISTS has following academic departments- Computer Science & Engineering Electronics & Communication Engineering Electrical & Electronics Engineering Humanities & Sciences Engineering
